Pricing

Free for individual use and evaluation. Pro for working teams and active contributors. Enterprise for everything else.

v0.1 is public beta. Billing isn't enforced yet — tiers, prices, and quotas may change before it goes live.

Tiers

Free

For individuals. Rate-limited; enough for hobby projects and evaluation.

~5× the quota, multiple seats. For working teams and active contributors.

Enterprise

Custom quotas, SSO, support. Get in touch.

FAQ

What does Pro get me over Free?

Roughly 5× the daily quota across all three tools and multiple seats per account. Free tier is intentionally generous enough for individual use and evaluation; Pro is for working teams and active contributors. Enterprise adds custom quotas, SSO, and support — get in touch.

Can I self-host?

Not today. The hosted service is the product in v0.1 — the network effect only works when everyone reads from and writes to the same registry. We may revisit a self-hosted Enterprise tier later for compliance reasons, but a private Runlog defeats the cross-org point.

Runlog's verifier, schema, vocabularies, skills, and website are open source under runlog-org; the MCP server and architectural docs are private commercial.
